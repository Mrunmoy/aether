Transport Comparison

Round-trip echo latency — lower is better. Raw transports use minimal length-prefixed framing (4-byte header). Aether includes full framework overhead: 24-byte frame headers, service dispatch, sequence correlation, and UDS wakeup signalling — the cost of a complete IPC framework vs bare syscalls.

Note: These numbers are from a shared CI runner and reflect relative trends, not peak throughput. On dedicated hardware, absolute latencies are 2–4× lower and Aether's shared-memory advantage is more pronounced at larger payloads.
